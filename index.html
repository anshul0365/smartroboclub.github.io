About Us

Welcome to SmartRobo Club

At SmartRoboClub, we design, build and develop innovative things with the help of modern technologies.

In this era, robotics plays a significant role in our day to day life, whether it is our primary need or a business or industrial requirements. Today, robots are playing a vital role in simplifying our task. Taking this in our minds, we at Rama University formed a robotics club named SmartRoboClub for students and teachers.

This club was established virtually in the period of the pandemic COVID-19 for helping the first-line staff by giving a carrier robot that will deliver the food and basic necessity to the patient.

We are open for all, no matter what age, gender, and skills you possess.

Projects

Things we build up!

Carrier Robot

A walkable robot that will carry the essential stuff which are to be provided to the quarantined patient like food, medicine, water, etc. Seeing the current situation, currently all these essentials are provided by nurses or paramedical staff which might get the staff infected. In order to control this pandemic, we’ll use this robot so that no human contact will be made (until not necessary) to the patient suffering from COVID-19 which decreases the possibility of getting a greater number of people infected.

Smart Rover

A wifi-enabled rover (typically car) which is controlled by a mobile device through a web browser. This rover contains an ESP8266 wi-fi module through which the transmission of signals takes place. When the user gives command from its mobile device, this wi-fi module receives the signals. It relays it to the other parts of the rover i.e., D.C motors for accelerating the car, changing the position, or whether it is for backward motion. This rover runs on a rechargeable battery of 6V to 12V.
The word 'Smart' used here because this rover automatically detects for the object behind its path and stops at the position.
